What is THE ORACLE?

THE ORACLE is a free Korean fortune-telling (Saju) web application available at linefortune.com. Saju (사주), or the Four Pillars of Destiny, is a traditional Korean divination system based on the year, month, day, and hour of a person's birth. THE ORACLE uses this ancient system to generate personalized destiny readings for K-culture fans worldwide.

Features

  • Fortune Reading (운세): Enter your birth date, body weight, and MBTI to receive a personalized Korean Saju destiny reading.
  • Compatibility Check (궁합): Compare two people's birth information to determine romantic or friendship compatibility.
  • Group Reading (그룹): Get collective fortune readings for K-pop groups or groups of friends.

Languages Supported

Korean (한국어), English, Indonesian (Bahasa Indonesia), Spanish (Español).

Is it free?

Yes. THE ORACLE is completely free to use with no account or registration required.

Cultural Background

THE ORACLE draws from Korean shamanic tradition (무속 신앙), the Saju system rooted in Chinese cosmology, and presents comparative insights from Brazilian Candomblé and Indonesian Javanese mysticism through its blog section.
