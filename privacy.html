Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2026

Welcome to THE ORACLE ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at THE ORACLE.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information on our servers. The information you enter (birth date, weight, MBTI type) is processed entirely within your browser and is never transmitted to or stored by us.

We may collect anonymous usage data through third-party analytics services (see Section 3).

2. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Preference cookies: To remember your theme preference (dark/light mode), stored locally in your browser.
  • Analytics cookies: Set by Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors interact with our site (pages visited, time spent, etc.). This data is anonymous and aggregated.
  • Advertising cookies: If Google AdSense ads are displayed, Google may set cookies to show relevant ads based on your interests.

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data such as your IP address (anonymized), browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site. This information helps us improve our service.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Affiliate Links (Shopee)

Our website contains affiliate links to Shopee. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. Shopee may use cookies to track referrals. We clearly disclose affiliate links on the page.

For more information, please review Shopee's Privacy Policy.

5. Google AdSense

We may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party websites (Shopee, Twitter/X). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (including GDPR, LGPD for Brazil, or local regulations) to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Since we do not store personal data, these rights primarily apply to data held by our third-party service providers (Google, Shopee).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at medal2614@gmail.com. For privacy-related requests (data access, correction, or deletion), please include "Privacy Request" in the subject line and we will respond within 30 days.
