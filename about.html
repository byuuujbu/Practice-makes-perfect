About Us

✦ WHERE ANCIENT WISDOM MEETS MODERN CULTURE ✦

Welcome to THE ORACLE — your daily gateway to K-fortune, cosmic insight, and a touch of Korean mysticism, all wrapped in a modern digital experience available at linefortune.com.

We are a small, passionate team of K-culture enthusiasts, astrology lovers, and digital creators who believe that ancient wisdom should be accessible to everyone — no matter where you are in the world. Whether you're a longtime K-pop fan in Jakarta, a first-time saju seeker in São Paulo, or simply someone curious about what the universe has planned for you today, THE ORACLE was built for you.

Our Story

THE ORACLE was born from a simple question: what if fortune-telling could feel as exciting and shareable as K-pop itself? Korean fortune-telling traditions — saju (four pillars of destiny), the lunar calendar, and elemental theory — have guided people for thousands of years. Yet most of that wisdom remained locked behind language barriers and complex rituals.

We set out to change that. By blending the depth of traditional Korean divination with an experience that feels fresh, visual, and made for the social-media age, THE ORACLE brings cosmic guidance directly to your fingertips. Enter your birth date, discover your fate, and share your reading with friends — it's that simple.
